Welcome

Do you want to learn chess?

The reason why you have come to us is probably because you want to get better at chess?
Here you have the opportunity to learn chess from scratch or just improve your playstyle. The website is very user-friendly and can follow up on your requirements when it comes to chess lessons.

Here we also give you the option to create your own user to see your chess rating. This tells how good you are as a chess player.

General information about Chess

Chess consists of 2 players who have 16 pieces each; One has white pieces, while the other has black pieces. The pieces have different strength and mobility and have a starting position on the player's two bottom rows. The player with white pieces starts the game by making a move.
